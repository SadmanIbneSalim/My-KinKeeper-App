# 🤝 KeenKeeper

Ever realized you haven't talked to your best friend in weeks because life got in the way? Yeah, me too. That’s why I built **KeenKeeper**—a simple way to track when you last bothered your friends.

---

## 🚀 Check it out here
https://fancy-semifreddo-0d717e.netlify.app/

---

## 🤔 What does it do?
Basically, it’s a friendship tracker. You can log whenever you call, text, or video chat with your buddies. It keeps a history of everything so you can see who you're ignoring  and who you're talking to the most.

---

## 🔥 3 Cool Things It Does
* **Colorful Charts:** It shows you a cool donut chart of your chats. If you text too much and never call, the chart will totally expose you.
* **Never Forgets:** I used `localStorage`, so even if you refresh your browser or close the tab, your friendship history stays right there. 
* **Quick Logs:** Just click a button for 'Call', 'Text', or 'Video' and it instantly adds it to your timeline. Super fast.

---

## 🛠️ Stuff I Used
* **React** (The brain of the app)
* **Tailwind & DaisyUI** (To make it look pretty without much effort)
* **Recharts** (For that fancy donut chart)
* **Lucide React** (For the cute icons)
* **React Router** (To move between pages)

---

## 🛠️ How to run it on your computer
1. Clone this repo.
2. Open your terminal and type `npm install`.
3. Then type `npm run dev`.
4. Now , It's running.

---

## 📝 Just a Note
Since I’m using React Router, if you're on Netlify and you hit refresh on the Timeline page, it might freak out. To fix that, I added a `_redirects` file in the public folder. Problem solved!
